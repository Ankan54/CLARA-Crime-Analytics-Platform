How it works

A two-phase pipeline with the officer in the middle

Extraction is automatic. Filing is not. Every run pauses at a checkpoint until the investigating officer has reviewed the findings and resolved any identity matches.

Upload the case file

FIR, investigation reports, bank statements, UPI & chat screenshots. A new case always begins with an FIR — the system enforces it.

AIKYA reads everything

Vision and language models pick out names, numbers, accounts and transactions — then check every person against those already on record.

You review the findings

Entities, the money trail, a connection graph, and side-by-side "same person?" matches. Merge or keep separate — your call, recorded.

Proceed files it

On your approval, records enter case files, the case network and smart search. Originals are archived, untouched.
