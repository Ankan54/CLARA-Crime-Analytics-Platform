On this day, Arjun K, aged 21, a resident of Bengaluru and an engineering student, has come forward to report a case of online job/task fraud. Arjun states that he was approached through a Telegram message by an unknown individual promising easy income by completing simple online tasks such as rating products and liking videos. Initially, Arjun received small payments which encouraged him to invest larger amounts. He was subsequently asked to pay increasing deposits to unlock higher-tier tasks and eventual withdrawals. Following these instructions, Arjun transferred a total of Rs 3,50,000 across multiple transactions. However, after these payments, the operators stopped responding and the application became inaccessible.

Upon investigation, it was found that the mobile device used by the operator bears IMEI number 353816081234501. Additionally, the operator's internet connection was traced to IP address 103.74.19.141, which is geolocated to Electronic City, Bengaluru. Arjun has provided all necessary details and digital evidence to support his claim. He requests appropriate action be taken to recover his lost funds and to prevent further victims from falling prey to this scam.
