BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
The complainant Lakshmi Reddy, aged 22, a Unemployed Graduate from Mysuru, states that on 2026-06-19 they received a message on Telegram from a user named @tasks_support offering a 'simple online task job' with daily earnings of Rs. 2000-5000. The complainant was added to a Telegram group named Daily Earning Tasks and asked to complete simple tasks (liking videos, rating products) on an app called TaskMaster Pro. Initial small payments of Rs. 200-500 were received successfully, building trust. The complainant was then asked to 'upgrade to VIP membership' by depositing Rs. 5,000. After this, they were shown fake profits and asked to deposit increasingly larger sums to 'unlock withdrawals.' The complainant deposited a total of Rs. 29,834 across 4 transactions to UPI IDs dailywork003@fino, simpletask005@okhdfcbank and account 4440988776655003. When the complainant attempted to withdraw, the app showed an error and the group admins stopped responding. Phone numbers 3538160812 used by the operators are now unreachable.

During the course of the investigation, technical analysis revealed that the fraudulent application was hosted on the IP address 103.74.19.142. Further financial inquiry led to the identification of an additional beneficiary bank account numbered 4440988776655005, which was utilized for laundering the proceeds of the crime. Additionally, a secondary mobile number, 353816081234505, was linked to the fraudulent Telegram group administration and used to coordinate the scam. Efforts are underway to trace the real-time location of the accused operating these digital footprints.
