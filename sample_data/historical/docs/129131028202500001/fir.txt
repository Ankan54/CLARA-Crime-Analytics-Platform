BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
On 09/08/2025, at approximately 14:30 hours, I, Sandeep Gaonkar (39 years), a trader residing in Vijayapura, appeared before this station to report a cyber fraud incident. The incident occurred earlier that day when I attempted to sell goods to an individual posing as a customer. The individual expressed interest in making a payment via UPI and requested my contact details to initiate the transaction.

Instead of transferring the funds, the accused deliberately sent a "collect" request to my mobile number. Simultaneously, the accused confused me by claiming there was a technical issue requiring me to scan a specific QR code to verify the incoming payment. Misled by the urgency and the fraudulent instructions, I inadvertently authorized the transaction, assuming I was receiving funds. Consequently, a sum of Rs. 24,706 was debited from my bank account instead of being credited.

Upon checking my transaction history, I realized the fraud. The payment was processed through the VPA `sandeepg91@okicici`. The transaction reference ID recorded for this fraudulent debit is `BG82261993550`. Further investigation into the transaction details revealed the fraud was linked to the mobile number `9669996947`. I have not received the goods' payment, nor has the debited amount been reversed. I request the registration of an FIR and necessary legal action against the unknown accused for cheating and committing cyber fraud under the relevant sections of the law.
