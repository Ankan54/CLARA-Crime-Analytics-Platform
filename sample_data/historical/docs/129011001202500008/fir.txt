BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
I, Geetha Lamani, aged 20 years, a fresher residing in Kodagu, hereby submit this complaint regarding a job scam. On 2025-08-07, I came across an online advertisement from a placement agency promising immediate employment opportunities in a reputed multinational company. As a recent graduate seeking employment, I contacted the agency to inquire about the recruitment process.

The fraudsters, operating under the guise of a legitimate recruitment firm, convinced me that I was selected for a high-profile position, subject to the payment of mandatory registration and documentation fees. Trusting their professional demeanor and assurance of a guaranteed job, I proceeded to make the payments as instructed. The fraudsters insisted on immediate fund transfers to process my application and generate an offer letter. Consequently, I transferred a total of 352,779 INR in multiple tranches to the account details provided by them.

The transaction identifiers provided to me during the payment process included the UPI ID geethala18@okicici and the reference number BG51222558104. All communications regarding the payment were conducted through the mobile number 9922295834. After the final transaction, the agency ceased all communication, and I realized the job offer was fictitious. I have been cheated of my savings under the false pretext of employment. I request that a case be registered against the unknown fraudsters for their criminal acts of cheating and misappropriation of funds.
