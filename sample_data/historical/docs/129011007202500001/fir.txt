BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
On the afternoon of August 15, 2025, I, Shanthi Naidu, aged 31, a businessman residing in Bengaluru Urban, fell victim to a sophisticated UPI fraud. I had listed a piece of furniture for sale on an online marketplace and received a call from an individual posing as a prospective buyer. The caller expressed immediate interest and insisted on making an advance payment to secure the item.

To facilitate the transaction, the fraudster asked me to share my UPI ID. I provided him with my identifier, **shanthin93@okicici**. The caller claimed he was facing technical difficulties sending the money directly and requested that I accept a "collect" request or scan a QR code he sent via WhatsApp to verify my account. Trusting his explanation, I opened my UPI application.

Instead of receiving funds, the interface prompted me to enter my UPI PIN for what I believed was a verification process. The moment I entered the PIN, I received a shockingly high debit alert. I realized I had been tricked into authorizing a payment of ₹1,36,308. Upon reviewing the transaction history, I observed that the funds had been instantly transferred to the fraudster’s linked account.

The transaction details on my bank statement clearly reference the beneficiary VPA and mobile number used in the scam: **9856064317** and the transaction reference ID **BG77291767160**. The fraudster subsequently blocked my number and disconnected all communication. I am filing this complaint to report the cybercrime and request an immediate investigation into the beneficiaries of this transaction to recover my lost funds.
