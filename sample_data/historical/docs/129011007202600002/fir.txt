BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
The complainant Harish Desai, aged 21, a Unemployed Graduate from Bengaluru Urban, states that on 2026-06-22 they received a message on Telegram from a user named @tasks_support offering a 'simple online task job' with daily earnings of Rs. 2000-5000. The complainant was added to a Telegram group named Daily Earning Tasks and asked to complete simple tasks (liking videos, rating products) on an app called TaskMaster Pro. Initial small payments of Rs. 200-500 were received successfully, building trust. The complainant was then asked to 'upgrade to VIP membership' by depositing Rs. 5,000. After this, they were shown fake profits and asked to deposit increasingly larger sums to 'unlock withdrawals.' The complainant deposited a total of Rs. 49,749 across 8 transactions to UPI IDs worker006@icici, earnfast001@paytm, quickjob004@oksbi, taskpro002@airtel and account 4440988776655006. When the complainant attempted to withdraw, the app showed an error and the group admins stopped responding. Phone numbers 3538160812 used by the operators are now unreachable.

During the course of the investigation, the cyber cell traced the server IP address utilized for hosting the fraudulent application to 103.74.19.142. Further financial analysis revealed that the funds were rapidly transferred through a complex maze of bank accounts to obfuscate the money trail. Specifically, the illicit proceeds were moved from the initial collection points to accounts numbered 4440988776655001, 4440988776655004, and 4440988776655002. Additionally, call detail records analysis identified another mobile number, 353816081234501, which was frequently in communication with the primary suspects and was used to coordinate the scam operations. The complainant requests that a case be registered and legal action be taken against the perpetrators.
