BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
The complainant Kantharaju Venkataraman, aged 23, a Engineering Student from Bengaluru Urban, states that on 2026-06-10 they received a message on Telegram from a user named @tasks_support offering a 'simple online task job' with daily earnings of Rs. 2000-5000. The complainant was added to a Telegram group named Daily Earning Tasks and asked to complete simple tasks (liking videos, rating products) on an app called TaskMaster Pro. Initial small payments of Rs. 200-500 were received successfully, building trust. The complainant was then asked to 'upgrade to VIP membership' by depositing Rs. 5,000. After this, they were shown fake profits and asked to deposit increasingly larger sums to 'unlock withdrawals.' The complainant deposited a total of Rs. 59,522 across 6 transactions to UPI IDs simpletask005@okhdfcbank, dailywork003@fino, jobapp007@hdfcbank and account 4440988776655005. Further investigation revealed linked accounts 4440988776655003 and 4440988776655007 used for layering funds. Technical analysis traced the fraudulent application’s server activity to IP address 103.74.19.142. When the complainant attempted to withdraw, the app showed an error and the group admins stopped responding. Phone numbers 3538160812 and 353816081234505 used by the operators are now unreachable. A case is registered under relevant sections of the IT Act and IPC for cheating and impersonation. Investigation is taken up.
