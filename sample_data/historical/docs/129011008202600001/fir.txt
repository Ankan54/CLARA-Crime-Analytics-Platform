BRIEF FACTS / COMPLAINT NARRATIVE
The complainant Renuka Lamani, aged 18, a Student from Bengaluru Urban, states that on 2026-06-10 they received a message on Telegram from a user named @tasks_support offering a 'simple online task job' with daily earnings of Rs. 2000-5000. The complainant was added to a Telegram group named Daily Earning Tasks and asked to complete simple tasks (liking videos, rating products) on an app called TaskMaster Pro. Initial small payments of Rs. 200-500 were received successfully, building trust. The complainant was then asked to 'upgrade to VIP membership' by depositing Rs. 5,000. After this, they were shown fake profits and asked to deposit increasingly larger sums to 'unlock withdrawals.' The complainant deposited a total of Rs. 43,708 across 4 transactions to UPI IDs jobapp007@hdfcbank, earnfast001@paytm and account 4440988776655007. When the complainant attempted to withdraw, the app showed an error and the group admins stopped responding. Phone numbers 3538160812 used by the operators are now unreachable.

During the course of the investigation, a forensic analysis of the mobile application and transaction logs was conducted. Technical analysis revealed that the fraudulent application was hosted on the IP address 103.74.19.142. Further scrutiny of the financial trail identified additional beneficiary accounts used to siphon off the defrauded amounts. These included the bank account number 4440988776655001 and another virtual payment address linked to the number 353816081234505. The complainant verified these details from her transaction history. A case is registered under relevant sections of the IT Act and IPC, and further investigation is in progress to nab the accused.
